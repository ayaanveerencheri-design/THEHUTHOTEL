Our story

It's not just where you sleep — it's how you feel while you're here.

Since 2016, The Hut has welcomed guests into a thoughtfully designed boutique home in Kimihurura — a serene neighbourhood tucked behind the Kigali Convention Center. Our promise is the quiet luxury of a place that knows you, with the warmth of a Rwandan welcome.

From the panoramic terrace of our rooftop restaurant to the calm of the Ingoro pool lounge, every corner is shaped to soothe and to surprise.

"A stay with us is more than a break — it's a carefully crafted experience where every detail is designed to soothe your soul and elevate your journey."

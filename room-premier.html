Premier Room · 16 rooms available

Spacious, light-filled, and built for the long view.

Our most popular room — king or twin configurations, valley views, and interconnecting options for families travelling together.

The Premier Room is The Hut's everyday luxury. A king-sized bed (or twins, on request) sits against a softly lit headboard, with hand-woven Rwandan textiles layered into the bedding. Wide windows frame the valley beyond the Kigali Convention Center.

Interconnecting Premier Rooms are available for families or small groups travelling together — ask at reception when you book.

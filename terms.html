Last updated: May 2026

1. Booking and reservation

All reservations made directly with The Hut Boutique Hotel are subject to availability and confirmation. A valid form of identification (passport or national ID) and a credit card guarantee may be required to confirm your booking. Reservations are non-transferable.

2. Check-in and check-out

Standard check-in time is from 2:00 PM. Standard check-out time is 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur an additional charge. Guests must be at least 18 years of age to book a room.

3. Rates and payment

All published rates are in US Dollars (USD) and include applicable taxes and service charges unless otherwise stated. Rates include daily breakfast and complimentary Wi-Fi. Payment can be made by cash, major credit cards, or bank transfer.

4. Cancellation policy

For most flexible rates, reservations may be cancelled free of charge up to seven (7) days prior to the scheduled arrival date. Cancellations made within 7 days of arrival, no-shows, or early departures may be charged the full amount of the stay. Non-refundable rates are charged in full at the time of booking and are not eligible for refund. Please refer to your specific booking confirmation for exact terms.

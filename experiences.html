FAQs

Before you arrive.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM. Check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur an additional fee — please contact the front desk in advance.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes — private airport transfers are available on request as a paid service. Book in advance with our reservations team with your flight details.

Is breakfast included?

Breakfast is included in most room packages unless stated otherwise. It's served buffet-style at the main restaurant, with continental and à la carte options also available.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Complimentary high-speed fibre Wi-Fi is available in all guest rooms, public areas, and across the property. You'll receive login details at check-in.

Is the hotel family-friendly?

Yes — we welcome families. Baby cots are available on request, and some of our rooms can accommodate families with children. Many of our experiences are suitable for younger guests.

What is your cancellation policy?

For most rates, bookings may be cancelled free of charge up to 7 days prior to arrival. Non-refundable rates are charged in full at booking. Please refer to your booking terms for specifics.

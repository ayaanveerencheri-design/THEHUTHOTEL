The Rooftop

Where the city falls away.

"The view of the hills of Kigali is breathtaking. We would return." — Joan Z., Google Review

Our rooftop restaurant is the hotel's quiet centrepiece — open to the sky, framed by Kigali's silhouette. Chefs Sajith and Sadith lead a menu drawn from Rwandan ingredients and international classics, with thoughtful vegetarian and vegan options. The hour before sunset is a small ritual we'd recommend.

Junior Suite · 2 rooms available

An intimate retreat, two balconies, one quiet morning.

Our most intimate stay — a queen-sized retreat with two private balconies overlooking the Kigali Convention Center and the valley beyond.

The Junior Suite is The Hut's most personal room. A queen-sized bed sits at the heart of a soft, layered space, with a lounge corner that opens onto the long balcony — the perfect place to begin or end the day.

Designed for couples, weekenders, and travellers who want a little more room to breathe — the Junior Suite is also our most-requested room for honeymoons and anniversaries.

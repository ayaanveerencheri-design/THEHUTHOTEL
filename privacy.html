Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

The Hut Restaurant & Boutique Hotel ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or stay with us as a guest.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number, and postal address when you make a reservation, fill in our contact form, or subscribe to communications.
  • Reservation details — check-in and check-out dates, room preferences, number of guests, and any special requests.
  • Payment information — handled securely by our payment provider; we do not store full card details.
  • Identification — passport or ID number where required by Rwandan law during check-in.
  • Website analytics — anonymous usage data such as browser type, device, pages visited, and time on site, collected via standard web technologies.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your reservation and provide hotel and restaurant services.
  • Respond to your enquiries and customer service requests.
  • Send booking confirmations, updates, and (with your consent) marketing communications.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and tax obligations in Rwanda.
  • Improve our website, services, and guest experience.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the hotel (payment processors, booking platforms, IT providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Government authorities where required by Rwandan law.
  • Legal advisors and auditors where necessary.

5. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described above and to comply with legal requirements. Reservation records are typically retained for seven years for tax and accounting purposes.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. You may also withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" or by contacting us directly. Please email reservation@thehuthotelrwanda.com to exercise any of these rights.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and anonymous analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the website.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed completely secure.

9. Children

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.

11. Contact us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your information, please contact us at:
